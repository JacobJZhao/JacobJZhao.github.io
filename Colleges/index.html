Oregon State Univeristy

Oregon State Univeristy has two campuses, with the primary campus located in Corvallis, Oregon. In addition to 200+ academic programs, OSU features 14 experiment stations and 36 Extension offices. OSU is commited to "engaging in exceptional research, discovery, and innovation", and instills in their graduates critical thinking skills, a thirst for knowledge, and determination to impact the world. OSU offers CS pathways of various difficulties, from simple sensor boards to cloud-based datacenters. OSU does not specialize in any specific area of CS, but instead provides its students with a basic foundation of CS knowledge.
